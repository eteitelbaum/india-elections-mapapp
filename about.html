Use the slider underneath the map to your right to choose a year. The map will then display which party or coalition won the latest election in each state or constituency at the time. The "+" sign in the legend signifies that the winning party was part of a coalition. Click on the region to display its name.

State legislative assembly[1]: state borders have changed over time (e.g. 2000 & 2014), the map takes those changes into account and uses historically accurate borders.

Lok Sabha (available soon)[2]: election winners for the elections to the lower house of India's bicameral Parliament
